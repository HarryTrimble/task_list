Learn driving rules

Follow this guidance to learn the rules for driving:

You must:

  • drive safely by following the The Highway Code
  • drive a in safe vehicle
  • insure your vehicle
  • tax your vehicle
  • update the address on your licence
  • check if you can drive with a medical condition
  • renew your licence photo every 10 years

You can't drive a:

  • lorry
  • vehicles that weigh more than 3,500kg
  • bus
  • bus
  • minibus

You can drive:

  • by yourself
  • with passengers
  • on motorways
  • a car
  • a moped
  • a motorbike (provisionally)
  • a with a trailer (provisionally)
