Learn driving rules

You now have a full driving licence, this means you can drive:

  • by yourself
  • with passengers
  • on motorways
  • a car
  • a moped
  • a motorbike (provisionally)
  • with a trailer (provisionally)

You can't drive a:

  • lorry
  • bus
  • minibus
  • vehicles that weigh more than 3,500kg

Before you start driving, you must:

  • insure your vehicle
  • tax your vehicle
  • drive in a safe (MOT'd) vehicle
  • check if you need to report a medical condition

When you're driving, you must:

  • drive safely by following the The Highway Code
  • renew your licence photo every 10 years
  • know how penalty points work
