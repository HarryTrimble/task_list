Rules for practicing driving

Practice driving

To practice driving in order to pass your test, there are rules to follow.

You must:

  • practice in an insured car
  • have a provisional licence
  • practice with a driving instructor or fully licenced driver
  • ensure the people you practice with are insured on the car you practice in

You can't:

  • drive on the motorway

You can:

  • practice driving at night
  • practice without a driving insturctor, as long as you have a fully licenced driver with you, who is insured on the same car
  • take your driving test in your own car
  • drive with other people in your car
