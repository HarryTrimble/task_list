Become a transport manager

To transport goods in the UK, you need someone to be your transport manager.

Tasks to become a transport manager:

  1. do Road Haulage Operations training
  2. read guidance to apply to DVSA
  3. print application form
  4. fill in form
  5. send to DVSA
  6. get transport manager certificate
